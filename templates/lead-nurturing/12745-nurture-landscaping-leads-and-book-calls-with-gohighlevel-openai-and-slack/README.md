# Landscaping Lead Nurture & Booking Automation

Automate your landscaping business’s lead follow-up and booking with this AI-powered GoHighLevel workflow. Designed by Hyrum Hurst, AI Automation Engineer at QuarterSmart, this template takes every new lead and:

- Sends personalized SMS and email messages using OpenAI
- Logs lead activity in Google Sheets
- Notifies your Slack team of hot leads
- Schedules follow-ups based on engagement
- Generates weekly activity reports

This workflow is perfect for landscapers who want to increase lead conversion, save time, and scale operations without hiring extra staff. By combining GoHighLevel, AI personalization, Slack, and Google Sheets, this automation handles the entire lead lifecycle from first touch to booking.
