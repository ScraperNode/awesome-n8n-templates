# **Automated Webinar Signup Verification & Personalized Welcome Kit**

---

## **Description**

Transform your webinar registrations from basic form submissions into a **verified, personalized, and premium attendee experience**.

This intelligent workflow automates **webinar signup intake, email verification, attendee validation, AI-powered personalization, welcome kit generation, PDF creation, and automated email delivery** — ensuring only genuine attendees are onboarded while delivering a high-quality first impression at scale.

No manual checks, no fake signups, and no generic emails — just clean data and tailored communication.

---

## **What This Workflow Does**

Turns a simple webinar signup into a fully automated onboarding pipeline:

📝 **Capture Webinar Signup** – Collects attendee details such as name, email, company, and session preferences.

📧 **Email Verification** – Validates the attendee’s email address in real time to prevent fake or mistyped signups.

🚫 **Invalid Signup Handling** – Stops unverified registrations and alerts organizers instantly.

📊 **Verified Attendee Storage** – Stores confirmed attendee data in Google Sheets for tracking and reporting.

🧠 **AI-Powered Personalization** – Generates a customized welcome message based on attendee details and interests.

📄 **Welcome Kit Generation** – Creates a professional welcome document and converts it into a PDF.

📧 **Automated Email Delivery** – Sends the personalized welcome kit directly to the attendee.

📚 **Optional Extra Materials** – Dynamically generates and emails advanced learning resources when requested.
