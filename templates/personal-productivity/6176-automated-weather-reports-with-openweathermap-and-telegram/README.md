## Description

## Get a fun and clear weather report sent to your phone automatically! 📰
---
![Screenshot 20250719 at 5.34.17 PM.png](fileId:1809)
---
This little bot wakes up, checks the weather for you, and builds a super simple summary of your day.

## What it does:

🌡️ Grabs the current temperature and what it actually feels like.

📉 Figures out the high and low for the whole day.

📅 Gives you a 5-day forecast so you can plan your week.

✈️ Sends it all to you in a clean Telegram message!

## Setup is easy:
Just plug in your info for Telegram, add your location, and you're good to go! ✨

## 🔗 Nodes Used

OpenWeatherMap, Telegram, Schedule Trigger
