# 📊 Fetch Threads analytics for your latest posts and email an HTML report

> ⚡ **29 views** · 📊 [Market Research & Insights](../)

## Description

Fetch up to 100 of your latest Threads posts along with their insights 
(views, likes, replies, reposts, quotes), build a formatted HTML summary 
table, and send it to your inbox automatically.

Supports both manual runs and scheduled execution (every 2 days by default).
