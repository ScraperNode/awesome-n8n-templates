# ⚡ Generate instant google meet links with a slack command

> ⚡ **4,060 views** · ⚡ [Personal Productivity](../)

## Description

### How it works
This template uses a slack app to connect with your google calendar, generate an instant google meet link and post it as a message in a slack channel

![slackmeetcommand.png](fileId:763)

#### Setup steps
1. Firstly, you'll need to create a slack app 
2. Authenticate and connect your slack account
3. Connect and choose the Google calendar you want to generate Google meet links for
4. Customize your slack message 
5. Then using a `/meet` command in slack, you can instantly generate and post your Google meet links

## 🔗 Nodes Used

Slack, Webhook, Google Calendar
