# Pet Grooming Appointment & Client Engagement Automation

Automate reminders, follow-ups, and client engagement for your pet grooming business with this simple n8n workflow by Hyrum Hurst, AI Automation Engineer at QuarterSmart.

- Sends personalized SMS and email reminders with grooming prep tips
- Tracks client confirmations and responses in Google Sheets
- Notifies groomers of daily schedules via Slack
- Sends post-appointment thank-you or reschedule messages
- Compiles weekly engagement reports automatically

Perfect for pet groomers looking to reduce no-shows, increase client satisfaction, and save time. Combines AI, Slack, Google Sheets, SMS, and Email for end-to-end automation.
