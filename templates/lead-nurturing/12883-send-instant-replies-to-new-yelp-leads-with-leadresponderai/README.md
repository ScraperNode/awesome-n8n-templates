## Description

Automate replies for new Yelp leads. This workflow helps small businesses win clients through immediate engagement the moment they reach out and increasing revenue by 30%.

### How it works
Yelp is monitored every minute for new leads. Once a lead is detected, an automated reply is sent immediately.

### Setup steps
First, sign up at https://app.leadresponder.ai/ to obtain an API key. Then, enter the key into the workflow settings.

### Customization
Upgrade from static messages to AI-generated personalization. Additionally, you can automate your scheduling by sending availability in your response.

## 🔗 Nodes Used

HTTP Request, Schedule Trigger

Download [`workflow.json`](workflow.json) and import into n8n:
