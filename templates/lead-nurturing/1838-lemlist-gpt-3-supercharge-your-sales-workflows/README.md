# 💬 lemlist <> GPT-3: Supercharge your sales workflows

> ⚡ **3,469 views** · 💬 [Lead Nurturing & AI Sales Agents](../)

## Description

Use GPT-3 to classify email responses in lemlist.

And automate:

- Slack alerts when a lead is interested
- the creation of tasks when a lead is OOO
- unsubscription of leads when they request it

## 🔗 Nodes Used

HTTP Request, Slack, HubSpot, Lemlist, Lemlist Trigger, OpenAI
