## Description

## Send Webflow form data to Google Sheets 🚀  
### How It Works  
This workflow connects your Webflow form submissions to Google Sheets with **style and efficiency**. Here's the magic in action:  
1. **Form Submitted** 🎉 – A Webflow form submission triggers the flow.  
2. **Fields Prepared** 🛠️ – Data gets organized, and a submission date is added.  
3. **Data Logged** 📋 – Your data finds a cozy new home in Google Sheets.  

---

### Set-Up Steps  
⏱️ **Estimated Time:** 5-10 minutes  
1. **Connect Webflow** 🌐 – Hook up your Webflow account and disable legacy APIs (instructions inside the workflow).  
2. **Set Up Google Sheets** 🧾 – Choose your spreadsheet and authenticate.  
3. **You're Ready!** 💫 – Let the workflow take it from here.  

---

### Why You'll Love It ❤️  
- **No More Manual Copy-Paste:** Save time and keep data clean.  
- **Smart Field Mapping:** Automatically creates columns and fits your data.  
- **Helpful Notes Along the Way:** Friendly tips guide you through setup like a pro.  

---

Enjoy the smooth ride from Webflow to Google Sheets! 🚀
