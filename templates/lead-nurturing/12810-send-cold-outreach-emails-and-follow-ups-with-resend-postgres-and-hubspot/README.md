# 💬 Send cold outreach emails and follow-ups with Resend, Postgres and HubSpot

> ⚡ **70 views** · 💬 [Lead Nurturing & AI Sales Agents](../)

## Description

Automate your cold email outreach with this 3-stage workflow that sends emails, triggers follow-ups after 3 days, and syncs responses to HubSpot CRM automatically.

- **Uses:** n8n, Postgres/Supabase, Resend API, HubSpot

- **Features:** Scheduled triggers, automatic follow-ups, response tracking via webhooks, CRM auto-sync

Perfect for sales teams and marketers who want to scale outreach without manual work.
