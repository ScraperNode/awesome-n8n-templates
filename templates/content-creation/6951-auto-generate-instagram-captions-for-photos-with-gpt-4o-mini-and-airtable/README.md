# 🎬 Auto-generate Instagram captions for photos with GPT-4o-mini and Airtable

> ⚡ **46 views** · 🎬 [Content Creation & Video](../)

## Description

Effortless Captions for Your Social Photos – Fully Automated

Save time and keep your content fresh. This workflow automatically grabs photos from a Google Drive folder, analyzes them using AI (OpenAI GPT-4o-mini), writes an engaging, Instagram-ready caption, and stores everything neatly in Airtable. Once used, the photo is moved to a separate folder so you never repeat posts.

🔹 Zero coding needed
🔹 Works with Google Drive, OpenAI & Airtable
🔹 No emojis, hashtags, or fluff—just solid, engaging captions
🔹 Perfect for content creators, marketers, and social media teams

Built on n8n – open source, customizable, and easy to extend.

## 🔗 Nodes Used

Airtable, Google Drive, Google Drive Trigger, OpenAI

## 📥 Import

Download [`workflow.json`](workflow.json) and import into n8n:
**Workflow menu → Import from File**
