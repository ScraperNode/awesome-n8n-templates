# 📱 Tiny tiny RSS (aka tt-rss) feed to Mastodon

> ⚡ **1,601 views** · 📱 [Social Media & Email Marketing](../)

## Description

Every 10 minutes look at your published news in your Tiny tiny RSS public feed and make a toot on your mastodon.

You'll need:

- Your mastondon URL instance
- Your mastondon access token
- Your Tiny Tiny RSS public published feed URL

## 🔗 Nodes Used

Cron, Function, HTTP Request, RSS Read, Start

## 📥 Import

Download [`workflow.json`](workflow.json) and import into n8n:
**Workflow menu → Import from File**
