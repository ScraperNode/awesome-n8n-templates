# 🔬 Track tenant document compliance with GPT-4,  Drive, Sheets, email and Slack

> ⚡ **6 views** · 🔬 [Document Extraction & Analysis](../)

## Description

## Who’s it for
Property management teams handling leases, IDs, and compliance documents.

## What it does
Automates document validation, reminders, escalation, and compliance reporting using AI and Google Sheets.

## How it works
Uploaded documents are analyzed for completeness, logged, and monitored until compliant.

## Setup
Connect Webhook, Drive, Sheets, AI, Email, and Slack.

## Customization
Change reminder timing, add more document types, or expand validation rules.

Created by QuarterSmart — Hyrum Hurst

## 🔗 Nodes Used

Send Email, Google Sheets, Slack, Webhook, Google Drive, Schedule Trigger
