# ⚡ Monitor Strava and send email updates

> ⚡ **1,136 views** · ⚡ [Personal Productivity](../)

## Description

Allow your friends to get updates when you do not meet your workout goals so that they can help you get to where you need to be!

Monitors your [Strava](https://www.strava.com/) account and sends an email to three accountability partners each day if you have not had enough activity so that they can reach out to you with some encouragement.

![image.png](fileId:384)

Make sure that you configure the Accountability Settings to meet your needs along with the *Strava* and *Send Email* credentials.

## 🔗 Nodes Used

Cron, Send Email, Start, Strava

## 📥 Import

Download [`workflow.json`](workflow.json) and import into n8n:
**Workflow menu → Import from File**
